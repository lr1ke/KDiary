About Kiez Diary


Journaling certainly isn't a new self-care practice - it's become a wellness mainstay over the past few years.
But what if we journal together? Could it broaden our consciousness? Could it promote self-reflection and idenfication with otherness at the same time?

How it works: Users share their location at the time they write into the journal. All entries are anonymous and listed together.

Every day a Kiez tells its story through the people living in it. Let's publish it.
